During my tenure at Universal Creative, Super Nintendo World stands out as a pinnacle achievement in my career, seamlessly merging theme park and video game design into a captivating whole. My involvement in this groundbreaking project began in 2017 and continued until the opening of the Osaka location in 2021. In 2023, Super Nintendo World made its debut in Los Angeles, with an Orlando version on the horizon. As a software engineer and prototyper, I played a crucial role in shaping the interactive elements of these projects. Notably, my most significant contribution was to the Bowser Jr Shadow Showdown interactive experience, where I was responsible for both prototyping and production code. It's worth mentioning that this experience boasts one of the most impressive skeletal tracking experiences I've encountered. Beyond my individual contributions, my work involved collaborating with various teams and external vendors, resulting in deliverables that exceeded expectations throughout the development of Super Nintendo World.

Nestled within Universal Studios Hollywood, the Secret Life of Pets: Off The Leash attraction offers a delightful experience for the whole family, immersing visitors in the charming world of Pets from Illumination Entertainment. This endeavor marked my first project when I joined Universal Creative as an Associate Software Engineer in 2017. I kick-started the development by crafting the initial prototype for the interactive "Puppy Mirror," a crucial element of the attraction that was later approved for implementation. My role extended to creating several additional prototypes for subsequent iterations, ensuring that the interactivity lived up to its potential. Furthermore, I played a key role as a consultant during the production phase, collaborating closely with the vendor responsible for bringing the final product to life. The Secret Life of Pets: Off The Leash represents a journey that began with a simple prototype and evolved into an engaging interactive element in this beloved family-friendly attraction.

"Project Clean 'Em Up" is a dynamic and innovative video game that seamlessly blends the nostalgia of classic collectathon platformers from the 90s with the adrenaline-pumping action of side-scrolling shoot 'em ups. With a heartfelt inspiration drawn from ocean conservation, the game follows the journey of a brilliant young Afro-Latina protagonist living on a vibrant Caribbean island. As she invents a remarkable cleaning robot, players embark on a globe-trotting adventure to combat pollution and preserve our oceans.

Black History Radio is my interactive art project for Black History Month, designed to be an engaging experience that transports users through the rich tapestry of decades in Black History. This custom creation utilizes a Sony Spresense microcontroller, with a vintage-style radio to bring the influential and historical figures of Black history to life. Showcased at the Universal Creative artist exhibition, this project marked my debut in the realm of public interactive art, underlining its significance as a transformative endeavor that celebrates and educates on the heritage and contributions of Black individuals throughout history.
