Bowser Jr: Shadow Showdown
Bowser Jr is a up to 12 player multisensory experience that is unlocked when 3 challenge keys are obtained
Players battle Bowser Jr; swatting bob-ombs, jump and ducking to avoid bullet bills, flinging fire balls, and more
Computer vision is leveraged to project a perfect shadow
combined with skeletal tracking to immerse the player into the experience
Bowser Jr Shadow Showdown Overview Video
Project I contributed to the most in SNW
UX Designer and I developed a user friendly design tool for the game design team.
Featured all of the proposed game mechanics, including skeletal tracking
Dozens of configurable parameters per game element
Savable configurations, allowed the game designers to play test preset configs
Recorded Analytics, allowing the game design team to use data with playtest results for fine tuning
Timeline tool enables designers to determine length of expereinece and when elements come into play, if at all
User Friendly interfaced encouraged leadership to provided feedback directly with the design tool
In addition to the design tool I wrote production code with the projects contracted vendors
Ensured the experience was well optimised along every stop of the pipeline
Gamplay programming and optimization
Jumped in the last 6 months of production and adapted quickly to the codebase and pipeline
